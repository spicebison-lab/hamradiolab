01 Information We Collect

Ham Radio Lab collects two types of information: information you provide directly, and information collected automatically when you visit the site.

Information You Provide

  • Email address — only if you voluntarily sign up for the newsletter or quick-start guide
  • Contact form submissions — name and email if you reach out via a contact form

Information Collected Automatically

  • Pages visited and time spent on them
  • Referring website (how you found this site)
  • Browser type and operating system (aggregated, not individually tracked)
  • General geographic region (country/state level — not precise location)

Note: This site does not collect your name, phone number, address, or any payment information. If you purchase gear through an affiliate link, that transaction happens entirely on the retailer's site under their privacy policy.

02 How We Use Your Information

Information collected on this site is used only for the following purposes:

  • To send the newsletter or free guides you signed up for
  • To understand which content is useful so more of it can be written
  • To respond to questions or messages you send
  • To improve site performance and fix broken pages

Your information is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

03 Cookies & Tracking

This site uses cookies and similar tracking technologies. Here's what's in use:

Cookie Type Purpose Can Be Opted Out?
Analytics (Google Analytics) Aggregated page view and traffic data Yes — browser settings or GA opt-out
Affiliate tracking (Amazon, etc.) Tracks clicks on affiliate links for commission attribution Yes — browser settings
Email service Tracks newsletter open rates and link clicks (if subscribed) Yes — unsubscribe at any time

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Note that some site features may not work correctly with cookies disabled.

04 Third-Party Services

This site uses third-party services that have their own privacy policies. Ham Radio Lab does not control how these services collect or use data:

  • Google Analytics — website traffic analysis. Google Privacy Policy ↗
  • Amazon Associates — affiliate link tracking when you click product links. Amazon Privacy Policy ↗
  • Ham Radio Outlet — affiliate link tracking when applicable. Subject to HRO's own privacy policy.
  • Email service provider — used to send the newsletter. Your email is stored on their platform.

When you click an affiliate link and visit a retailer's site, you are subject to that retailer's privacy policy, not this one.

05 Email & Newsletter

If you sign up for the Ham Radio Lab newsletter or free guide, your email address is stored with a third-party email service provider and used to send you the requested content and occasional updates.

  • You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email
  • Your email is never shared with advertisers or other third parties
  • You will only receive content related to ham radio — no unrelated promotions

06 Your Rights & Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data. Regardless of location, this site honors the following:

  • Access — you can ask what data is held about you
  • Deletion — you can request that your email or submitted data be deleted
  • Opt-out — you can unsubscribe from emails or opt out of analytics cookies at any time
  • Correction — you can request correction of any inaccurate information

To exercise any of these rights, contact me directly using the information at the bottom of this page.

07 Data Retention

Email addresses are retained for as long as you remain subscribed to the newsletter. Upon unsubscribing, your email is removed from the active list. Analytics data is retained in aggregated, anonymized form indefinitely.

Contact form submissions may be retained for up to 12 months for reference and follow-up purposes.

08 Children's Privacy

Ham Radio Lab is not directed at children under the age of 13. This site does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal data to this site, please contact me and it will be deleted promptly.

09 Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.

Significant changes will be noted in the newsletter if you are subscribed.
